About me

I’m Yongjing, a Software Engineer with both Back-End and Front-End experience. Now I am working in the U.S. I love building beautiful things with usability and performance.

In 2013, I came to the Pennsylvania State University to continue my graduate study. I felt so proud of myself because I completed my major(Electrical Engineering) and minor(Computational Science) in a 2-year master of science program. Also, I was the first guy to get a job offer in my class.

In 2015, I started working as a Research Software Engineer one week after my graduation ceremony at VideoMining, where I learned to research about indoor localization algorithm, build real-time data pipeline in Amazon Web Services ecosystem and other interesting Internet of Things projects. I really appreciate this experience in VideoMining.

In 2016, I joined CG Max Design as Web Developer because I am interested in Front-End interactive projects which can help me to become a Full-Stack Software Engineer in future.

Why I like coding

Software is the ultimate interface between hardware improvement and human usage. Since users can only get benefits from the improvement of good software, I wanna be this interface with my coding skills to make people live better.
